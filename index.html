A collaborative researcher network examining the disparities & drivers of eviction.

Formally the Evictions Study, ERN collects and analyzes eviction data across the U.S., highlighting the hard work of researchers We specialize in using advanced data science & highlighting the work of researchers across the United States and working closely with . We collect data and help researchers analyze theirs, specializing in using data science techniques to "messy data" into usable formats and collects data is a collaborate with researchers across the United States to collect and analyze court and Sheriff records to enumerate and identify race, ethnic, and sex disparities of evicted renters. Our goal is to expand public and scholarly knowledge by identifying the prevalence and drivers of evictions in under-studied regions, providing evidence-based research for policy reform .
